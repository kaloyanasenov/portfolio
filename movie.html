"The choice"

This project was created for the university course “Audio and visual production”. The requirements were for a 5 minute long movie, that should include a character named Sam, who is a barista and a specific quite. For the duration of the movie a character should experience a moment of realisation. The movie was shot using iPhone 14pro and audio is captured with Rode wireless Go 2, while everything was edited on DaVinci Resolve 19.
