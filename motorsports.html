Motorsports projects

My passion for motorsports certainly deserves a separate page where I can showcase some of the projects I have worked on during university related to the sport.

3D character animation

This project was part of an assessment for 3D character animation, where a model was fully constructed from the ground up, then rigged and animated by using Blender. The assessment brief required a showreel, demonstrating an idle, walking, fighting, dancing and emoting animations, I went for an additional driving animation as well, in order to have a complete product. For my character, of course I went ahead with Fernando Alonso, he was the first that popped up in my mind when I heard we have complete freedom with choosing a character and that character needs to have an emote animation. The inspiration for the final model is from the Funko Pop models of other F1 drivers, including Max, Lewis, Valtteri, etc.

F1 statistics data visualisation

This project was made for a Data visualisation coursework in my last university year. The requirements were to link at least 3 data sets, that are not fully related, so I went with F1 team popularity, points and profit, to find if success equals popularity and profit, which is actually not always the case as it turns out.

The data was scraped from the financial documents of F1 teams uploaded on gov.uk, while other information was collected from the official standings-such as results and points, or official fan surveys for team and driver popularity. The collected data was handled in Excel, then visualised with Tableau.
