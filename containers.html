Student accommodation UI/UX application

Link to the Figma file

Tools:
Figma, Design Thinking, User Personas

Requirements:
Requirements were Low, Mid and High fidelity design, with testing in each iteration, furthermore, we were required to follow the Design Thinking model- Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype, Test.

Approach and development:
I had this assessment when I studied in the Netherlands and we had freedom for the topic, as long as we follow the requirements. At that time I lived in a unique student accommodation that was constructed from cargo containers. There were several issues in the living place, 300+ students and only 2 washing machines, hard to communicate in just one group chat as a lot of spam could be generated, sometimes your bike would break down and you won’t have the tools or knowledge to fix it. These were just some examples of problems we faced.
